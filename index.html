Project Mission Statement

My name is Louis Wynne, and before 2024 I had very little understanding of AI.

After a life-changing period, during which I immersed myself in AI, I realized I wanted to embark upon a journey. My goals may be naively ambitious, but I feel compelled to see how far toward them I can aspire. This project, hosted on GitHub under the name AEOHA (which stands for "an excess of human assets?"), is my personal record of this journey. The title is borrowed from a short story of mine, which reflects some of the philosophical tensions with humanity gripped by fear and excitement in almost equal measure at the advent of AI in our society. The story casts forward to a future world in which AI has become the central guiding force on our planet. While nobody can know how far towards this reality we will ever progress, it is already clear that AI literacy will be an essential requirement of future success. With this aim in mind, I have set the first waypoint of my journey. Become a competent data analyst. I believe by developing skills in data analysis I will begin developing the skills necessary to deploy my own AI resources at some future time when my skill and knowledge will allow this.

Key Data Points for Understanding the Project

Mission and Vision:

The primary objective of the project is inspired by the belief that symbiotic relationships between humanity and future generations of AI will become the norm. The objective therefore is to position myself as an early adopter of this new reality. I therefore feel that any test of authorship, questioning "did a human or an AI" write this, create this, edit this, etc.; will soon be largely irrelevant. Much of what follows is created with, by, or in consultation with the advice of AI. However, the use of a GitHub repository is intended to demonstrate my newfound knowledge (albeit obtained at a much quicker rate than would have previously been possible) is valid, and by the structure and presentation I hope to convince you, the reader, that at the time of my application I am confident and capable in the necessary disciplines. This should allow me to progress a career in the sector and begin to gain the knowledge, experience, and intuition necessary to progress further to higher levels of competency.

The Role of Empathy and Data:

As a highly empathetic person, I hope to apply this quality to data analysis. Rather than focusing only on technical details, I believe that understanding the intention behind data, and the context it exists in, is crucial. AI should not just be a mechanism we control but a partner that helps us grow and understand. My empathy will guide my understanding of data, and through this, I aim to develop insights that can benefit others.
